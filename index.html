About Us

Welcome to a world where everyone can dress in style at a reasonable price

We carry new and gently used clothing, shoes, handbags, and accessories from designer name brands. Find styles and colors that will not only reflect your personality but your own fashion sense as you peruse our selection. Prada, Burberry, Tahari, and Mulberry are some of the high end fashion labels that are seen regularly in this little store. Lots of ongoing sales and automatic markdowns are available, resulting in steals that are scarce amid such a high end stock!

Our selective approach ensures that you won't need to spend a lot of time searching through outdated styles, and ensures that your purchases will be classic additions to your closet. Pick any item and we will help you put an ensemble together within your own budget. Women's sizes available from 0 to 18w!

We are now pleased to offer a men's line as well, with designer brands including Lacoste, Ralph Lauren, Vineyard Vines, Tommy Bahamas, Nautica, and more. Come in and find a new spring style or bring in your own merchandise to consign!
